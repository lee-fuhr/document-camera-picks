Document cameras have become essential tools for remote teaching, online presentations, and even cooking tutorials. They let you share physical documents, objects, or whiteboard sketches with crystal clarity, far better than a laptop’s webcam. When choosing one, focus on resolution (most good models are HD or 4K), autofocus speed, and build quality. Budget matters, but even moderately priced options can produce sharp images.
